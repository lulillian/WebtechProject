History

Belgian Malinois orginated from the Malines area of Belgium in the 1800s. They were bred to be herding and working dogs and are often used in the military and police force today. They are often confused with German Sheperds, however their coat is much shorter and their face is more narrow.

Characteristics

  • High Energy
  • Very Playful
  • Easy to Train
  • Highly Intelligent
  • High Prey Drive
  • Family Friendly
